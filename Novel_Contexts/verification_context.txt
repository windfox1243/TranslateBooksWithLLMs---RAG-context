# CHARACTERS & GENDERS
- Li Fan: Male, elder brother of Li Meimei.
- Li Meimei: Female, younger sister of Li Fan.
- Uncle Wang: Male, older neighbor.

# RELATIONSHIPS & ADDRESSING FORMS
- Li Fan to Li Meimei: Addresses her as "em" or "em gái" (pronoun pair: anh - em).
- Li Meimei to Li Fan: Addresses him as "anh" or "anh trai" (pronoun pair: em - anh).
- Li Fan and Li Meimei to Uncle Wang: Address him as "Bác Wang" or "Chú Wang".

# GLOSSARY & TERMINOLOGY
- brother: anh (when Meimei speaks to Li Fan), anh trai (when referring to Li Fan)
- sister: em (when Li Fan speaks to Li Meimei), em gái (when referring to Li Meimei)
